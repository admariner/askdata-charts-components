Feeds

A Feed is a curated group of Askdata Data Cards, the simplest piece of content that can be exchanged on Askdata.. You can create your own Feeds or follow Feeds created by others. Viewing a Feed timeline will show you a stream of Data Cards from only the accounts on that List.

You can access the Feeds Section by clicking Feed on the left.

Dataset

You can connect different data sources to your workspace from multiple data sources. Every datasource has its own special features. You can connect your datasource and start querying using Natural Language. Our team developed clever algorithms that will automatically transform your words into the result you were looking for!

Head over to the Dataset section and add yours!
